Early 1970s: Personal Commitment to the Homeless

In the early 1970s, Buddy Gray began inviting homeless individuals into his own apartment to shield them from the dangers of the streets. This personal mission laid the foundation for what would become a more structured effort to assist the homeless population in Cincinnati.
